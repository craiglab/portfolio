ABOUT ME

I like to craft experiences that make people smile. Or at the very least get a little hit of dopamine. I like solving problems to make life a little easier and enjoyable for users. I use Figma, Djay Pro, and other tools to make ideas come to life.

With an 8-year career in Human Resources and a BS degree in Psychology, I've dedicated myself to understanding, empathizing, and engaging with people. My work in HR allowed me to hone skills in communication, problem-solving, and human-centered thinking. Meanwhile, my studies in Psychology deepened my insights into human behavior and cognitive processes. This blend of experiences naturally led me to the field of UX design, where I see a profound opportunity to create intuitive and meaningful user experiences. My transition into UX is not just a career change; it's a continuation of my passion for connecting with people and shaping interactions that resonate. Having recently completed a comprehensive UX/UI bootcamp, I am eager to apply my unique blend of skills to design products that are not only functional but also empathetic to user needs.
